Hi! my name is kalie gachnang. i’m a freelance graphic designer, and here is a brief history of me!

i grew up in wisconsin rapids, wi, and in 2007 i enrolled at the university of wisconsin - madison with the plan the pursue a career in the medical field. by the end of my freshman fall semester i knew that science totally wasn’t for me and decided to focus on my love for art. in 2012 i graduated with my bfa in graphic design. aside from my graphics courses, i also focused on woodworking while in college, and chose that as my second area of concentration.

in january 2014, my husband and i moved to oakland, ca. i'm enjoying exploring my new surroundings and seeing all of the beautiful things the bay area has to offer. i love typography and lettering and often get the warm fuzzies when i see beautiful decorative swoops, swirls, and curls. i've also been spending my free time learning to design websites and i even wrote my own site from scratch.

i still very much enjoy woodworking and have recently found myself playing with hand carving and learning to use a lathe. other hobbies of mine include biking, camping, and snuggling with my pooch!
